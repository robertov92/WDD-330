Master Mobile UX

Key concepts to remember

  1. Rewire your brain for mobile first: I need to design for mobile devices first. The web started only with desktop computers in mind. However, mobile devices are skyrocketing the market, while desktop computers are declining in consumption.
  2. Understand Users: Users value simplicity, speed, and sometimes they 'don't know what they want, until you show it to them'(Steve Jobs).
  3. Strategic Menu Design: Nav bar can go to the bottom of the screen so users can reach it with their thumbs. Replace the hamburger button with a button labeled 'Menu.'
  4. Mobile-Friendly Forms: The narrator used some very cool animations to make the form very friendly. This video is worth watching again.
  5. Mobile Advertising User Experience: Try to get rid of as much advertising as possible because people visit your website for content, not for advertisment. This video is also worth watching again.
